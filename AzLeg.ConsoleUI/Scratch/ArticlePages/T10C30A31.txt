<p><font color=GREEN>10-3731</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Voting agreements</u></font></p>

<p>A. Two or more members may provide for the manner in which they will vote by signing an agreement for that purpose.</p>

<p>B. Unless otherwise provided in the voting agreement, a voting agreement created under this section is specifically enforceable. </p>
