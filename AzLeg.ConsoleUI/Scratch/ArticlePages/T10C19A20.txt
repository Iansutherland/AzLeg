<p><font color=GREEN>10-2020</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>License and other fees; tax exemption</u></font></p>

<p>Each association shall pay a nonrefundable annual license fee of ten dollars.  A nonprofit agricultural products association formed pursuant to this article shall be exempt from all franchise or license taxes imposed on its income. Each association shall pay a nonrefundable fee of ten dollars for filing articles of incorporation and a nonrefundable fee of two dollars fifty cents for filing an amendment to the articles.  All other for-profit association filing, service and copying fees are determined pursuant to section 10-122 and all other nonprofit association filing, service and copying fees are determined pursuant to section 10-3122. </p>
