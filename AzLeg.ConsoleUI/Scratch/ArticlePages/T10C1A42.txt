<p><font color=GREEN>10-142</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Number of shareholders</u></font></p>

<p>A. For purposes of chapters 1 through 17 of this title, the following identified as a shareholder in a corporation's current record of shareholders constitutes one shareholder:</p>

<p>1. Three or fewer co-owners.</p>

<p>2. Any corporation, partnership, trust, estate or other entity.</p>

<p>3. The trustees, guardians, custodians or other fiduciaries of a single trust, estate or account.</p>

<p>B. For purposes of chapters 1 through 17 of this title, shareholdings registered in substantially similar names constitute one shareholder if it is reasonable to believe that the names represent the same person. </p>
