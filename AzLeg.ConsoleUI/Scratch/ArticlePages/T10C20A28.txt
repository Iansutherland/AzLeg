<p><font color=GREEN>10-2228</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Definition of disqualified shareholder</u></font></p>

<p>In this article, unless the context otherwise requires, &quot;disqualified shareholder&quot; means a shareholder described in subsection 10-2223, subsection A, paragraph 1 or 2 or the legal representative of that shareholder. </p>
