<p><font color=GREEN>10-2055</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Articles of incorporation</u></font></p>

<p>A. Articles of incorporation of a cooperative shall recite that they are executed pursuant to this article and shall state:</p>

<p>1. The name of the cooperative.</p>

<p>2. The address of its principal office.</p>

<p>3. The names and addresses of the incorporators.</p>

<p>4. The names and addresses of its directors.</p>

<p>B. The articles may also contain provisions not inconsistent with this article which are deemed necessary or advisable for the conduct of its business.</p>

<p>C. The articles shall be executed by an incorporator.</p>

<p>D. It is not necessary to recite in the articles of incorporation the purpose for which the cooperative is organized or to recite any of its corporate powers. </p>
