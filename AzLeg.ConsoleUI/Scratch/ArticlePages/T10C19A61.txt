<p><font color=GREEN>10-2061</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Nonliability of members for cooperative indebtedness</u></font></p>

<p>A member shall not be liable or responsible for the acts, debts, liabilities or obligations of the cooperative and the property of the members shall not be subject to execution therefor. </p>
