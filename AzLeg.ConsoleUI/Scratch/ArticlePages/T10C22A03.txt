<p><font color=GREEN>10-2403.</font> <font color=PURPLE><u>Incorporation of benefit corporation</u></font></p>

<p>A benefit corporation shall be incorporated in accordance with chapter 2, article 1 of this title, except that its articles of incorporation must also state that it is a benefit corporation. </p>
