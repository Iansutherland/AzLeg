<p><font color=GREEN>10-2246</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Revocation of certificate of authority</u></font></p>

<p>Pursuant to chapter 15, article 3 of this title, the commission may administratively revoke the authority of a foreign professional corporation to transact business in this state if a licensing authority with jurisdiction over a professional service described in the corporation's articles of incorporation certifies to the commission that the corporation has violated or is violating a provision of this chapter and describes the violation in the certificate. </p>
