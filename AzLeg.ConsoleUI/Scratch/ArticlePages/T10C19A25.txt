<p><font color=GREEN>10-2025</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Security law; applicability</u></font></p>

<p>Title 44, chapter 12 applies only to a cooperative association that is formed under this article if either:</p>

<p>1. The cooperative association is formed with capital stock.</p>

<p>2. The cooperative association exercises its powers pursuant to section 10-2005, paragraph 10. </p>
