<p><font color=GREEN>10-11004</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Class voting by members on amendments</u></font></p>

<p>The members of a class of a corporation are entitled to vote as a class on a proposed amendment to the articles of incorporation only if a class vote is provided for in the articles of incorporation or bylaws. </p>
