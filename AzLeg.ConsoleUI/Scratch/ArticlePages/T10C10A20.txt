<p><font color=GREEN>10-1020</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Amendment by board of directors or shareholders</u></font></p>

<p>A. A corporation's board of directors may amend or repeal the corporation's bylaws unless either:</p>

<p>1. The articles of incorporation or chapters 1 through 17 of this title reserve this power exclusively to the shareholders in whole or part.</p>

<p>2. The shareholders in amending or repealing a particular bylaw provide expressly that the board of directors may not amend or repeal that bylaw.</p>

<p>B. A corporation's shareholders may amend or repeal the corporation's bylaws even though the bylaws may also be amended or repealed by the board of directors. </p>
