<p><font color=GREEN>10-3723</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Voting requirements</u></font></p>

<p>Unless chapters 24 through 40 of this title provide otherwise, the articles of incorporation or the bylaws require a greater vote or voting by class, if a quorum is present, the affirmative vote of the votes represented and voting, for which affirmative votes also constitute a majority of the required quorum, is the act of the members. </p>
