<p><font color=GREEN>10-2019</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Annual reports</u></font></p>

<p>The association shall make an annual report on forms furnished by the corporation commission, containing a general statement of its business during the fiscal year showing:</p>

<p>1. The number of members and amount of membership fees received.</p>

<p>2. The total expense of operations.</p>

<p>3. Its indebtedness or liabilities, and its balance sheets. </p>
