<p><font color=GREEN>10-2003</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Requirements for formation; association profits</u></font></p>

<p>A. The following may form a cooperative association with or without capital stock under this article: </p>

<p>1. Five or more persons engaged in the production of products or services.</p>

<p>2. Five or more small businesses, individuals or entities engaged in the marketing and distribution of manufactured goods for import and export purposes.</p>

<p>B. Associations organized under this article may make profits for themselves or for their members. </p>
