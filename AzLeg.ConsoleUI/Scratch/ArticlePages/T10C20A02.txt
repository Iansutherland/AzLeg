<p><font color=GREEN>10-2202</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Application of Arizona business corporation act</u></font></p>

<p>Chapters 1 through 17 of this title apply to professional corporations, both domestic and foreign, to the extent they are not inconsistent with the express provisions of this chapter. </p>
