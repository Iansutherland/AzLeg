<p><font color=GREEN>10-3622</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Purchase of memberships</u></font></p>

<p>Except as provided in the articles of incorporation or bylaws, a corporation may purchase the membership of a member who resigns or whose membership is terminated for the amount and pursuant to the conditions set forth in or authorized by its articles of incorporation or bylaws. A corporation shall not make a payment that violates section 10-11301 or 10-11302. </p>
