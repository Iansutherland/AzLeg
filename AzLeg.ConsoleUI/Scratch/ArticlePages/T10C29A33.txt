<p><font color=GREEN>10-3633</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Stay of proceedings</u></font></p>

<p>If the corporation commences an inquiry into the allegations made in the demand or complaint, the court may stay any derivative proceeding for such period as the court deems appropriate. </p>
