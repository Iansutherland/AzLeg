<p><font color=GREEN>10-2082</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Taking of acknowledgments by officer or member</u></font></p>

<p>A person authorized to take acknowledgments under the laws of this state shall not be disqualified from taking acknowledgments of instruments executed in favor of a cooperative or to which it is a party, by reason of being an officer, director or member of the cooperative. </p>
