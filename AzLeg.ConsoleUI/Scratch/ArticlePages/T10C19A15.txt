<p><font color=GREEN>10-2015</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Referral to membership</u></font></p>

<p>Upon demand of one half of the entire board of directors any matter that has been approved or passed by the board shall be referred to the membership or the shareholders for decision at the next special or regular meeting. </p>
