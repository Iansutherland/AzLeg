<p><font color=GREEN>10-2073</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Merger, interest exchange, conversion, domestication or division; definitions</u></font></p>

<p>A. A domestic electric cooperative nonprofit membership corporation may be a party to or otherwise undertake a merger, an interest exchange, a conversion, a domestication or a division by complying with chapter 34 of this title and title 29, chapter 6.Â  A plan must be approved by two-thirds of the members.</p>

<p>B. If a domestic electric cooperative nonprofit membership corporation is to be created in a merger, a conversion, a domestication or a division, both of the following apply:</p>

<p>1. The initial members of the corporation must consist of five or more natural persons or two or more cooperatives in accordance with section 10-2053.</p>

<p>2. The name of the corporation must comply with section 10-2054.</p>

<p>C. For the purposes of this section:</p>

<p>1. &quot;Conversion&quot; means a transaction authorized by title 29, chapter 6, article 4.</p>

<p>2. &quot;Division&quot; means a transaction authorized by title 29, chapter 6, article 6.</p>

<p>3. &quot;Domestication&quot; means a transaction authorized by title 29, chapter 6, article 5.</p>

<p>4. &quot;Interest exchange&quot; means a transaction authorized by title 29, chapter 6, article 3.</p>

<p>5. &quot;Merger&quot; means a transaction authorized by title 29, chapter 6, article 2.</p>

<p>6. &quot;Plan&quot; means a plan of merger, interest exchange, conversion, domestication or division. </p>
