<p><font color=GREEN>10-741</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Standing</u></font></p>

<p>A shareholder may not commence or maintain a derivative proceeding unless the shareholder both:</p>

<p>1. Was a shareholder of the corporation at the time of the act or omission complained of or became a shareholder through transfer by operation of law from one who was a shareholder at that time.</p>

<p>2. Fairly and adequately represents the interests of the corporation in enforcing the right of the corporation. </p>
