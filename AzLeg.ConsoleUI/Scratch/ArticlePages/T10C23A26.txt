<p><font color=GREEN>10-2726</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Rights of action</u></font></p>

<p>An acquiring person, an issuing public corporation and shareholders of an issuing public corporation may sue at law or in equity to enforce the provisions of this article. </p>
