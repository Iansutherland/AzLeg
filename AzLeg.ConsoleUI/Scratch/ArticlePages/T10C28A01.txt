<p><font color=GREEN>10-3501</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Known place of business and statutory agent</u></font></p>

<p>Each corporation shall continuously maintain in this state both:</p>

<p>1. A known place of business that may be the address of its statutory agent.</p>

<p>2. A statutory agent who may be either:</p>

<p>(a) An individual who resides in this state.</p>

<p>(b) A domestic business or nonprofit corporation formed under this title.</p>

<p>(c) A foreign business or nonprofit corporation authorized to transact business or conduct affairs in this state.</p>

<p>(d) A limited liability company formed under title 29.</p>

<p>(e) A limited liability company authorized to transact business in this state. </p>
