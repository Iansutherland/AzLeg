<p><font color=GREEN>10-1872</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Classifications of business trusts</u></font></p>

<p>Business trusts shall be either domestic or foreign. A domestic business trust is a business trust organized under the laws of this state. A foreign business trust includes every other business trust. </p>
