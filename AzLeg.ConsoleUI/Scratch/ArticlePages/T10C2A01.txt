<p><font color=GREEN>10-201</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Incorporators</u></font></p>

<p>One or more persons may act as the incorporator or incorporators of a corporation by delivering articles of incorporation and a certificate of disclosure to the commission for filing. </p>
