<p><font color=GREEN>10-2266</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Dissolution or liquidation</u></font></p>

<p>In the event of dissolution or liquidation of a corporation none of the corporation's assets shall be distributed to the shareholders until all sums due the members of the corporation as creditors have been paid in full. </p>
