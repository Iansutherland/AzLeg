<p><font color=GREEN>10-301</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Purposes</u></font></p>

<p>Subject to any limitations or requirements contained in its articles of incorporation or in any other applicable law, a corporation shall have the purpose of engaging in and may engage in any lawful business activity. </p>
