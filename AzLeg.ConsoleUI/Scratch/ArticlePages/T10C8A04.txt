<p><font color=GREEN>10-804</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Election of directors by certain classes or series of shareholders</u></font></p>

<p>A. If the articles of incorporation authorize dividing the shares into classes or series, the articles may also authorize the election of all or a specified number of directors by the holders of one or more authorized classes or series of shares.</p>

<p>B. One or more classes or one or more series of shares entitled to elect one or more directors is a separate voting group for purposes of the election of directors. </p>
