<p><font color=GREEN>10-2221</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Shares and professional corporation status on shares</u></font></p>

<p>Notwithstanding section 10-627, no statement or notice of any restrictions imposed by this chapter on the transfer of shares of a professional corporation is required to appear on any share certificate issued by a professional corporation or to otherwise be provided to the shareholders of a professional corporation. </p>
