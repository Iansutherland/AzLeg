<p><font color=GREEN>10-11630</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Certificate of good standing; license and registration renewal</u></font></p>

<p>If a state agency can confirm through the commission that an applicant for renewal of a license or registration is entitled to the issuance of a certificate of good standing at the time of the inquiry, the agency shall not require an applicant to obtain a certificate. </p>
