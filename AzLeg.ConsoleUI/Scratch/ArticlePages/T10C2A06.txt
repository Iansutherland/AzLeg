<p><font color=GREEN>10-206</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Bylaws</u></font></p>

<p>A. The board of directors of a corporation shall adopt initial bylaws for the corporation.</p>

<p>B. The bylaws of a corporation may contain any provision for managing the business and regulating the affairs of the corporation that is not inconsistent with law or the articles of incorporation. </p>
