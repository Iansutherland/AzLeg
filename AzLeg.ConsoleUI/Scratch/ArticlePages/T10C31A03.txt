<p><font color=GREEN>10-3803</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Number of directors</u></font></p>

<p>A. A board of directors shall consist of one or more individuals, with the number specified in or fixed in accordance with the articles of incorporation or bylaws.</p>

<p>B. The articles of incorporation or bylaws may establish a variable range for the size of the board of directors by fixing a minimum and maximum number of directors. If a variable range is established, the number of directors may be fixed or changed, from time to time, within the minimum and maximum, by the members or the board of directors. </p>
