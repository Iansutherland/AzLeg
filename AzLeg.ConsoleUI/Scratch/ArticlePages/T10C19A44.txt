<p><font color=GREEN>10-2144</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Filing of articles</u></font></p>

<p>Articles of incorporation, amendment, consolidation, merger, conversion or dissolution, when executed by the cooperative, shall be delivered to the corporation commission for filing. If the corporation commission finds that the articles presented conform to the requirements of this article, it shall, on the payment of the required fees, file the articles, and on filing, the incorporation, amendment, consolidation, merger, conversion or dissolution becomes effective. This section also applies to a certificate of election to dissolve as provided in section 10-2143, subsection B, paragraph 2. </p>
