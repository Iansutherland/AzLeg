<p><font color=GREEN>10-1702</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Application to qualified foreign corporations</u></font></p>

<p>A foreign corporation authorized to transact business in this state on January 1, 1996 is subject to chapters 1 through 17 of this title but is not required to obtain a new certificate of authority to transact business under chapters 1 through 17 of this title. </p>
