<p><font color=GREEN>10-11901</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Purposes for which corporation sole may be formed</u></font></p>

<p>Corporations may be formed to acquire, hold and dispose of church or religious society property for the benefit of religion, for works of charity and for public worship, and of property of scientific research institutions maintained solely for pure research and without expectation of pecuniary gain or profit, in the manner provided in this article. </p>
