<p><font color=GREEN>10-11603</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Scope of inspection rights; charge</u></font></p>

<p>A. A member's agent or attorney has the same inspection and copying rights as the member the agent or attorney represents.</p>

<p>B. The right to copy records under section 10-11602 includes, if reasonable, the right to receive copies made by photographic, xerographic or other means.</p>

<p>C. The corporation may impose a reasonable charge covering the costs of labor and material for copies of any documents provided to the member. The charge shall not exceed the estimated cost of production or reproduction of the records.</p>

<p>D. The corporation may comply with a member's demand to inspect the record of members under section 10-11602, subsection B, paragraph 3 by providing the member with a list of the corporation's members that was compiled no earlier than the date of the member's demand. </p>
