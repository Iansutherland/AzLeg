<p><font color=GREEN>10-2060</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Members in cooperative</u></font></p>

<p>Each incorporator of a cooperative shall be a member of the cooperative, but no other person may become a member unless the person agrees to use electric energy or other services furnished by the cooperative as the cooperative establishes in its bylaws. A husband and wife may hold a joint membership in a cooperative. Membership in a cooperative is not transferable, except as provided in the bylaws. The bylaws may prescribe additional qualifications, limitations, classifications and rights concerning membership. </p>
