<p><font color=GREEN>10-2079</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Fees</u></font></p>

<p>The corporation commission shall charge and collect the following nonrefundable fees for:</p>

<p>1. Filing articles of incorporation, ten dollars.</p>

<p>2. Filing articles of amendment, ten dollars.</p>

<p>3. Filing statement of merger, interest exchange, conversion, domestication or division, ten dollars.</p>

<p>4. Filing certificate of election to dissolve, ten dollars.</p>

<p>5. Filing articles of dissolution, ten dollars.</p>

<p>6. Filing certificate of change of principal office, five dollars. </p>
