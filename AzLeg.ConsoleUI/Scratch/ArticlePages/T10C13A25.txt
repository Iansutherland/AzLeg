<p><font color=GREEN>10-1325</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Payment</u></font></p>

<p>A. Except as provided in section 10-1327, as soon as the proposed corporate action is taken, or if such action is taken without a shareholder vote, on receipt of a payment demand, the corporation shall pay each dissenter who complied with section 10-1323 the amount the corporation estimates to be the fair value of the dissenter's shares plus accrued interest.</p>

<p>B. The payment shall be accompanied by all of the following:</p>

<p>1. The corporation's balance sheet as of the end of a fiscal year ending not more than sixteen months before the date of payment, an income statement for that year, a statement of changes in shareholders' equity for that year and the latest available interim financial statements, if any.</p>

<p>2. A statement of the corporation's estimate of the fair value of the shares.</p>

<p>3. An explanation of how the interest was calculated.</p>

<p>4. A statement of the dissenter's right to demand payment under section 10-1328.</p>

<p>5. A copy of this article. </p>
