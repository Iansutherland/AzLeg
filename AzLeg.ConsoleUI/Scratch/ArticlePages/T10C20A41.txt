<p><font color=GREEN>10-2241</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Termination of professional corporation status</u></font></p>

<p>A. If a professional corporation ceases to render professional services or otherwise elects to terminate its professional corporation status, it shall amend its articles of incorporation to:</p>

<p>1. Delete references to rendering professional services, unless as described in section 10-2213 the services may be rendered in the business corporation form.</p>

<p>2. Delete references to it being a professional corporation.</p>

<p>3. Conform its corporation name to the requirements of section 10-401.</p>

<p>B. After the amendment becomes effective, the corporation may continue in existence as a business corporation under chapters 1 through 17 of this title and it is no longer subject to this chapter. </p>
