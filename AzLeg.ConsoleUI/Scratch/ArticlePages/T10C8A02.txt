<p><font color=GREEN>10-802</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Qualification of directors</u></font></p>

<p>The articles of incorporation or bylaws may prescribe qualifications for directors. A director need not be a resident of this state or a shareholder of the corporation unless the articles of incorporation or bylaws so prescribe. </p>
