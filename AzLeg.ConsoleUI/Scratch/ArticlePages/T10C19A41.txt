<p><font color=GREEN>10-2141</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Conversion of cooperatives into generation and transmission cooperatives</u></font></p>

<p>A cooperative created pursuant to article 2 of this chapter and which meets the requirements of this article may be converted into a generation and transmission cooperative authorized to do business and entitled to all privileges and immunities of a generation and transmission cooperative organized pursuant to this article upon the adoption of a resolution to such effect by its board of directors and the submission of the resolution to the corporation commission. </p>
