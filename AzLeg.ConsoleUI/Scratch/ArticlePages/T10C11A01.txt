<p><font color=GREEN>10-1101</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Definitions</u></font></p>

<p>A. In this article, unless the context otherwise requires:</p>

<p>1. &quot;Plan&quot; means a plan of merger, interest exchange, conversion, domestication or division.</p>

<p>2. &quot;Transaction&quot; means a merger, an interest exchange, a conversion, a domestication or a division.</p>

<p>B. Except for terms defined in chapters 1 through 17 of this title or unless the context otherwise requires, terms used in this article have the same meanings prescribed in section 29-2102. </p>
