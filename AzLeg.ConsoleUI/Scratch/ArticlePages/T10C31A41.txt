<p><font color=GREEN>10-3841</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Duties and authority of officers</u></font></p>

<p>Each officer has the authority and shall perform the duties set forth in the bylaws or, to the extent consistent with the bylaws, the duties and authority prescribed by the board of directors or by direction of an officer authorized by the board of directors to prescribe the duties and authority of other officers. </p>
