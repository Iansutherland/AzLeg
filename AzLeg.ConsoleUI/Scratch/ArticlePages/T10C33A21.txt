<p><font color=GREEN>10-11021</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Amendment by board of directors or members</u></font></p>

<p>If the articles of incorporation or the bylaws require that an amendment to or repeal of the corporation's bylaws be submitted to the members, the procedures set forth in section 10-11003 shall apply. </p>
