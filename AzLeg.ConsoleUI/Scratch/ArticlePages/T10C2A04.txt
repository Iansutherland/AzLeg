<p><font color=GREEN>10-204</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Actions before incorporation; liability</u></font></p>

<p>Any person purporting to act as or on behalf of a corporation that knew or should have known that the corporation has not been formed under chapters 1 through 17 of this title is liable for all debts and liabilities incurred by the act. </p>
