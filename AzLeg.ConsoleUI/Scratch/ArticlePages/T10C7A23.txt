<p><font color=GREEN>10-723</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Shares held by nominees</u></font></p>

<p>A. A corporation may establish a procedure by which the beneficial owner of shares that are registered in the name of a nominee is recognized by the corporation as the shareholder. The extent of this recognition may be determined in the procedure.</p>

<p>B. The procedure may set forth:</p>

<p>1. The types of nominees to which it applies.</p>

<p>2. The rights or privileges that the corporation recognizes in a beneficial owner.</p>

<p>3. The manner in which the procedure is selected by the nominee.</p>

<p>4. The information that must be provided when the procedure is selected.</p>

<p>5. The period for which selection of the procedure is effective.</p>

<p>6. Other aspects of the rights and duties created. </p>
