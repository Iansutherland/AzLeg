<p><font color=GREEN>10-3128</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Certificate of good standing</u></font></p>

<p>A. A person may apply to the commission to furnish a certificate of good standing for a domestic or foreign corporation.</p>

<p>B. The certificate of good standing sets forth all of the following:</p>

<p>1. The domestic corporation's corporate name or the foreign corporation's corporate name used in this state.</p>

<p>2. That either:</p>

<p>(a) The domestic corporation is incorporated under the law of this state and the date of its incorporation.</p>

<p>(b) The foreign corporation is authorized to transact business in this state.</p>

<p>3. That all affidavits and annual reports required before the date of the certificate have been filed with the commission.</p>

<p>4. That all annual filing fees due before the date of the certificate have been paid.</p>

<p>5. That according to the records of the commission, the corporation is in good standing in this state.</p>

<p>C. Subject to any qualification stated in the certificate, a certificate of good standing issued by the commission may be relied on as conclusive evidence of the matters stated in the certificate. </p>
