<p><font color=GREEN>10-2012</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Organization of board of directors</u></font></p>

<p>The directors shall elect from their number a president and one or more vice-presidents, and a secretary and treasurer, who need not be members. The offices of secretary and treasurer may be combined. The funds of the association shall be deposited only as authorized by the board. </p>
