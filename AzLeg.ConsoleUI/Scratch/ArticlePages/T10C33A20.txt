<p><font color=GREEN>10-11020</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Amendment by board of directors</u></font></p>

<p>A. If a corporation has no members, its board of directors may adopt one or more amendments to the corporation's bylaws.</p>

<p>B. The adoption of an amendment pursuant to this section shall require the approval in writing by any person or persons whose approval is required pursuant to section 10-11030. </p>
