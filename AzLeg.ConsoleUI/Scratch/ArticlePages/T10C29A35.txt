<p><font color=GREEN>10-3635</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Discontinuance or settlement</u></font></p>

<p>No derivative proceeding may be discontinued or settled without the court's approval. If the court determines that a proposed discontinuance or settlement will substantially affect the interests of the corporation's members or a class of members, the court shall direct that notice be given to the affected members. </p>
