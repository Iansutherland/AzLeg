<p><font color=GREEN>10-3630</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Definitions</u></font></p>

<p>In this article, unless the context otherwise requires:</p>

<p>1. &quot;Derivative proceeding&quot; means a civil suit in the right of a domestic corporation or, to the extent provided in section 10-3637, in the right of a foreign corporation.</p>

<p>2. &quot;Independent person&quot; means a person with no personal interest in the transaction and no personal or other relationship which influences the person. </p>
