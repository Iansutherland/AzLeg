<p><font color=GREEN>10-1005</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Amendment before issuance of shares</u></font></p>

<p>If a corporation has not yet issued shares, an amendment to the articles of incorporation may be adopted by a majority of the members of the board of directors, unless the articles of incorporation require adoption by a greater number of the members of the board of directors. </p>
