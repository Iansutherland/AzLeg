<p><font color=GREEN>10-1871</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Definitions</u></font></p>

<p>In this article, unless the context otherwise requires:</p>

<p>1. &quot;Business trust&quot; means an unincorporated association or trust of the type which at common law was known as a &quot;business trust&quot; or &quot;Massachusetts trust&quot;, created by an instrument under which property is held and managed by trustees for the benefit and profit of such persons as are or may become the holders of transferable certificates evidencing beneficial interests in the trust estate. </p>
