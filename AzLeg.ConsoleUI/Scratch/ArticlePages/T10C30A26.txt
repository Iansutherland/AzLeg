<p><font color=GREEN>10-3726</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Other methods of electing directors</u></font></p>

<p>A corporation may provide in its articles of incorporation or bylaws the process for election of directors by members or delegates by any of the following means:</p>

<p>1. On the basis of chapter or other organizational unit.</p>

<p>2. By region or other geographic unit.</p>

<p>3. By preferential voting.</p>

<p>4. By any other reasonable method. </p>
