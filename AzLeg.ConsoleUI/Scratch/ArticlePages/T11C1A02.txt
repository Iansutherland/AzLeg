<p><font color=GREEN>11-102</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Definitions</u></font></p>

<p>In the statement of the boundaries in this article:</p>

<p>1. &quot;To,&quot; &quot;on&quot; or &quot;along&quot; a mountain range or mountains means the summit point or summit line of the mountain range of mountains.</p>

<p>2. &quot;Up&quot; or &quot;down&quot; a creek or river means the middle of the main channel thereof.</p>

<p>3. &quot;Mouth&quot; of a creek or river or &quot;junction&quot; of a creek or river with a river is the point where the middle of the channel of each intersects the other. </p>
