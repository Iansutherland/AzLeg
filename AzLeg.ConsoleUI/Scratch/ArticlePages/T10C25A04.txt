<p><font color=GREEN>10-3204</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Liability for noncorporate transactions</u></font></p>

<p>All persons purporting to act as or on behalf of a corporation with actual knowledge that no corporation exists under chapters 24 through 40 of this title are jointly and severally liable to the extent not precluded by section 12-2506 for all liabilities created while so acting. </p>
