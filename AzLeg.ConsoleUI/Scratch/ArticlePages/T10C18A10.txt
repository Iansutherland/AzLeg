<p><font color=GREEN>10-1810</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Definition of relative rights of capital units</u></font></p>

<p>&quot;Relative rights of capital units&quot; means all the rights, privileges, obligations and duties of the capital units and may include, but are not limited to, disproportionate variations of the following:</p>

<p>1. Participation in dividends or distributions from operating income.</p>

<p>2. Participation in dividends or distributions from income other than operating income.</p>

<p>3. Participation in distributions of the proceeds of a sale of all or substantially all of the assets of the corporation with further disproportionate variation depending upon the degree of gain or loss.</p>

<p>4. Participation in distributions upon liquidation or dissolution.</p>

<p>5. Voting rights.</p>

<p>6. Restrictions or limitations on transfer.</p>

<p>7. The obligation to perform services or provide goods or other property to the corporation.</p>

<p>8. The obligation to devote time and energies which are collateral to corporate purposes.</p>

<p>9. Assessments, if any. </p>
