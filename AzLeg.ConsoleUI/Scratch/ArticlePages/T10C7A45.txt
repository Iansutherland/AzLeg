<p><font color=GREEN>10-745</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Discontinuance or settlement</u></font></p>

<p>A derivative proceeding may not be discontinued or settled without the court's approval. If the court determines that a proposed discontinuance or settlement will substantially affect the interests of the corporation's shareholders or a class of shareholders, the court shall direct that notice be given to the shareholders affected. </p>
