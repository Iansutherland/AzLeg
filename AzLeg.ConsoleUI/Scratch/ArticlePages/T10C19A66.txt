<p><font color=GREEN>10-2066</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Change of location of principal office</u></font></p>

<p>A cooperative may, upon authorization of its board of directors or its members, change the location of its principal office by filing in the office of the corporation commission a certificate reciting the change of principal office, executed by the cooperative.</p>
