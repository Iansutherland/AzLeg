<p><font color=GREEN>10-11440</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Deposit with department of revenue</u></font></p>

<p>A. Assets of a dissolved corporation that should be transferred to a creditor, claimant or member of the corporation who cannot be found or who is not competent to receive them and does not have a legal representative who is legally competent to receive them shall be reduced to cash subject to known trust restrictions and deposited with the unclaimed property division of the department of revenue for safekeeping. </p>

<p>B. Notwithstanding subsection A, in the discretion of the unclaimed property division of the department of revenue property may be received and held in kind. </p>

<p>C. If the creditor, claimant, member or legal representative who is legally competent to receive the distributive portion furnishes satisfactory proof of entitlement to the amount deposited or property held in kind, the unclaimed property division of the department of revenue shall pay the creditor, member or legal representative that amount or property. </p>
