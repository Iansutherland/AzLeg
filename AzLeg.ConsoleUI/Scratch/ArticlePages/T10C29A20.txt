<p><font color=GREEN>10-3620</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Resignation</u></font></p>

<p>A. A member may resign at any time, except as set forth in or authorized by the articles of incorporation or bylaws.</p>

<p>B. The resignation of a member does not relieve the member from any obligations the member may have to the corporation as a result of obligations incurred or commitments made prior to resignation.</p>

<p>C. This section does not apply to corporations that are condominium associations or planned community associations. </p>
