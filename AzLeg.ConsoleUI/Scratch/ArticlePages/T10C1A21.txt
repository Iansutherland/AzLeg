<p><font color=GREEN>10-121</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Forms</u></font></p>

<p>A. Certificates, reports and statements required by section 10-202, subsection D and sections 10-1622 and 10-1623 to be delivered to and filed by the commission shall be made on the forms that are prescribed and furnished by the commission.</p>

<p>B. The commission may prescribe and furnish on request forms for other documents required or permitted to be filed by chapters 1 through 17 of this title, but their use is not mandatory. </p>
