<p><font color=GREEN>10-3637</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Applicability to foreign corporations</u></font></p>

<p>In any derivative proceeding in the right of a foreign corporation, the matters covered by this article are governed by the laws of the jurisdiction of incorporation of the foreign corporation except for sections 10-3633, 10-3635 and 10-3636. </p>
