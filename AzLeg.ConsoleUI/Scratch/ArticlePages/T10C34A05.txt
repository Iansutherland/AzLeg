<p><font color=GREEN>10-11105</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Statement of merger or interest exchange; publication or posting</u></font></p>

<p>Within sixty days after the commission approves the filing, either of the following must occur:</p>

<p>1. A copy of the statement of merger or interest exchange shall be published. An affidavit evidencing the publication may be filed with the commission.</p>

<p>2. The commission shall input the information regarding the approval into the database as prescribed by section 10-130. </p>
