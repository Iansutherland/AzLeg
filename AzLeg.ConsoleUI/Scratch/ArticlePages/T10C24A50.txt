<p><font color=GREEN>10-3150</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Private foundations; definition</u></font></p>

<p>A. Except if otherwise determined by a court of competent jurisdiction or to the extent no longer required by the internal revenue code of 1986, a corporation that is a private foundation as defined in section 509(a) of the internal revenue code of 1986 shall:</p>

<p>1. Distribute such amounts for each taxable year at a time and in a manner that does not subject the corporation to tax under section 4942 of the code.</p>

<p>2. Not engage in any act of self-dealing as defined in section 4941(d) of the code.</p>

<p>3. Not retain any excess business holdings as defined in section 4943(c) of the code.</p>

<p>4. Not make any taxable expenditures as defined in section 4944 of the code.</p>

<p>5. Not make any taxable expenditures as defined in section 4945(d) of the code.</p>

<p>B. For the purposes of this section, &quot;code&quot; means the internal revenue code of 1986 as amended. </p>
