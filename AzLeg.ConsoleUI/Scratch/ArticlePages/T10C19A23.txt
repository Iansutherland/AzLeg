<p><font color=GREEN>10-2023</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Inducing breach of contract; spreading false report; classification</u></font></p>

<p>A person knowingly inducing any member of an association to breach his marketing contract with the association, or who knowingly spreads false reports concerning the finances or management thereof, is guilty of a class 2 misdemeanor, and is also liable to the injured association in a civil action in the sum of five hundred dollars. </p>
