<p><font color=GREEN>10-2254</font>. <font color=PURPLE><u>Capital stock</u></font></p>

<p>The capital stock of a corporation shall be not less than one hundred thousand dollars to be evidenced by shares, having a par value of not less than ten dollars each. </p>
